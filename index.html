To the girl who keeps finding her way back to my heart ❤️

Before you read... please read till the end.

The Beginning

Every time you come back into my life, everything starts feeling normal again. I smile more. I wait for your messages. I become the version of myself that only exists around you.

The Truth

I wasn't completely myself—not because I cared less, but because I was scared.

The Biggest Misunderstanding

You may think I don't care. The truth is, I care more than I know how to show. My whole day depends on your mood.

The Plane Analogy

Imagine boarding a plane while already knowing it will crash someday. You can never truly enjoy the journey. That's how I felt. Every happy moment came with one thought: "What if she leaves again?"

What I Really Meant

When I said, "Come back when you're sure," I wasn't asking you to leave. I was asking you to stay.

One Last Thing

I opened my heart to you because I trusted you. Please don't make me feel like I shouldn't share my feelings with you next time. If I tell you my fears, it isn't because I want to push you away—it's because you're the one person I want to be honest with.

I'm Sorry

If my words hurt you, I'm truly sorry. Hurting you was never my intention. I was only afraid of losing you again.

Lastly...

I don't want temporary conversations. I don't want temporary care. I just want us. Peacefully. Honestly. Without fear. Without overthinking.

❤️ I wasn't asking you to come back.
I was asking you to stay.

— Akshay
